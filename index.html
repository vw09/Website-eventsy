About Me

Hey, I’m Sarah Van Wellen, a Graphic and Digital Media student with a passion for design and technology. In my free time, I sing every week in a choir with my sisters – something I truly enjoy. I also love being creative through drawing, and when I’m not sketching, you can probably find me behind my PC gaming. And yes, I’ll admit it… I have a slight Red Bull addiction and an energy level to match!

While organizing events for our choir, I often ran into problems. The tools we used didn’t really fit our needs. Too much scattered information and unclear communication made planning way harder than it needed to be. That’s what inspired me to create EVENTSY, an app that brings everything together in one place – exactly what I was missing.
